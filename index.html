with Zoho Writer's extensions

Zoho Writer offers a set of prebuilt extensions to help automate your business operations right within your documents.

The Zoho Writer advantage

Besides extensions, Zoho Writer offers various productivity-enhancing features:

  • Real-time sharing and collaboration with your peers
  • Options to insert images, tables, and fields in your documents
  • Advanced formatting and layout options for designing your documents
  • Built-in spelling and grammar checker, along with AI-powered writing suggestions
  • Powerful automation templates to fulfill your signing, fillable, and mail merge needs
  • Offline mode and a desktop app with full offline capabilities and no-signup usage
  • Android and iOS apps, and full access via mobile browsers so you can work on your documents anytime

  • Create and manage notes for your documents

    Create and share notes from your document or insert them directly into your document. You can also view, edit, and delete notes without moving away from the editor in Zoho Writer.
